Welcome to Florida Water Tours

Experience the Intracoastal Waterway at its finest featuring picture-perfect attractions. Based in St. Augustine, we offer amazing eco-tourism cruises for vacationers and locals
year-round. Offering private and public services, we
encourage you to explore the fun side of learning about
nature, history and wildlife through our exciting cruises.

What makes us unique?

  • Free parking! *Parking fills up quick on weekends and holidays so please arrive early, carpool or take a cab!
  • Restroom on board.
  • Bar on board!
  • A variety of different cruises to choose from.
  • St. Augustine’s only Wine Tasting on the water!
  • An experienced crew with tour guide and marine
    naturalist licenses.
  • The Salty Boutique! We have a great little gift shop for
    you to explore while you check in and wait for your tour!

Aside from all of those wonderful things, Florida Water Tours takes a percentage of EVERY TICKET PURCHASED and donates into our educational outreach program. This non-profit,
called Florida Water Warriors is something that we are very proud of! We take low income and under-served students on Educational Cruises, all in an effort to spark some passion
about marine wildlife, nature and conservation!
